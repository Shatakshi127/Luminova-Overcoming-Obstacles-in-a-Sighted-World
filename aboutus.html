ABOUT US

Welcome to Luminova

At Luminova, we are dedicated to enhancing the lives of visually impaired individuals and their families. Our mission is to create an inclusive digital information system that caters to two distinct audiences: those who are partially visually impaired and those who are completely visually impaired. With a passion for making a positive impact, Luminova strives to be a beacon of hope and empowerment in the world of visual impairment.



Luminova understands:
  • Empowerment
  • Advocacy
  • Inclusivity
  • Innovation
  • Community Building


Quality Policy of Luminova:

  • Is whole-heartedly committed to ensuring a healthy community.
  • Is dedicated to fulfilling needs by ensuring accessibility and ease of obtaining services.
  • Focuses on services, learning and development.
  • Is committed to diversity and social justice.


Vision of Luminova

  • Our mission is to empower visually impaired individuals to lead fulfilling lives by providing them with the resources, tools, and support they need to achieve their goals and aspirations.
  • We advocate for the rights and well-being of visually impaired individuals, promoting awareness and understanding of visual impairment to foster a more compassionate and supportive environment.
  • We aim to build a strong and supportive community of visually impaired individuals, their families, and stakeholders, fostering connections and providing a platform for sharing experiences, knowledge, and resources.
