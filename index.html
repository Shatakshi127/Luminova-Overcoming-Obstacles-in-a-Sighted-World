Welcome To Luminova

At Luminova, our purpose is to make a positive impact in the lives of visually impaired individuals and their families. We are driven by a commitment to empower, support, and advocate for a more inclusive world.


As a digital information hub, we offer a wealth of resources for parents and stakeholders of visually impaired individuals. From job opportunities to advocacy tools, we strive to be a trusted source of information and guidance.


Recognizing the uniqueness of each individual's needs, we provide personalized solutions in education, communication, entertainment, mobility, and daily living. Our aim is to foster independence and enrich lives.


Accessibility is at the core of our values. Our website is designed with a focus on inclusivity, ensuring that it caters to both partially visually impaired and completely visually impaired users.


We embrace innovation and collaborate with experts to develop cutting-edge solutions that enhance the lives of visually impaired individuals, promoting their participation in a technologically advancing world.


Luminova is more than just a platform; it's a supportive community. We encourage meaningful connections, resource sharing, and advocacy, uniting individuals with visual impairment in their journey towards empowerment.

Absolutely Confidential

Confidentiality is our esteemed code of ethics. Rest assured, any conversation with the counsellor will stay just there- with the counsellor. Unless life-threatening tendencies come forth, we maintain absolute confidentiality. We respect your privacy.
Integrity

Integrity involves maintaining the consistency, accuracy and trustworthiness of data over its entire lifecycle. Data must not be changed in transit, and steps must be taken to ensure data cannot be altered by unauthorized people (for example, in a breach of confidentiality).
