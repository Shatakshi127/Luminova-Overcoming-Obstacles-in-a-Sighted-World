ADVOCACY

LEGISLATION AND

RIGHTS

Legislation and rights for individuals with visual impairment, including the blind, encompass the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA), and the Rehabilitation Act, ensuring equal opportunities and accessibility.

ADVOCACY

ORGANIZATIONS

In India, advocacy organizations for the blind include the National Association for the Blind (NAB) and the Blind People's Association (BPA), working towards empowerment, education, and inclusive opportunities for visually impaired individuals.

RESOURCE

LIBRARY

A resource library for blind people offers accessible materials, such as braille books, audio materials, and assistive technology resources, catering to their specific needs and enhancing their knowledge and independence.

UPDATES AND

NEWS ALERT

The platform includes an update and newsletter feature, keeping users informed about the latest advancements, events, and resources relevant to visual impairment, fostering a connected and engaged community.

TECH

TALKS

The tech talks feature offers a series of informative and engaging discussions on technology advancements, accessibility, and innovations for visually impaired individuals, encouraging knowledge-sharing and community interaction within the platform.
